December 4, 2018

I’ve been publishing more non-fiction, in The Threepenny Review and Mr. Beller’s Neighborhood. These can be found in the Essays section.

But mostly I’ve been doing what I suspect we all have: trying to keep my lower lip above the rising tide of viciousness and hate that threatens to swamp our society. Let’s all hope these floodwaters crest and recede… soon.
